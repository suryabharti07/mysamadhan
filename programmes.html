Our Programmes

Education Initiatives

Setting up of School (Su-Shikshyalaya)

A School has been started in Fatehpur Panchayat of Raghopur block of Vaishali district by SK. Initially the Classes have been started from Nursery to Class III. The idea is to impart quality education to the children in this remote rural location. At present around 90 children are enrolled and sincere efforts are being made to prepare the academic base of the Children so that they can they can excel in the future academic career. The fee has been kept at bare minimum.The Organization has also purchased a piece of land in the area to build a High Class School in the future which will cater to the educational needs of the children of the area who are bereft of quality education.

Running of NFE(Non Formal Education) Centers (Aapna Vidyalaya)

The Organization is running 70 NFE(Non Formal Education) Centers in Raghopur block for children who do not even attend to Govt,which is completely free of cost. Schools. Batch of 25 children each are attending these Centers and the response has been overwhelming in the area. Mission is to build a solid academic base of the children coming to these Schools so that they can excel in their future academic journey.

Health & Sports Initiatives

Several areas in Vaishali district , where the Organization is working are flood prone areas. These areas are flooded by the river Ganga and Gandak very year.During such times of natural disaster,the Organization does its bit by making its contribution towards relief work. During post flood times, water related illness is commmon in the area as the flood waters gets mixed up with the ground water of the area.SK spreads awareness during such times among the local villagers to consume water by boiling it and also to properly clean their homes and their sorroundings after the flood waters recede from the area.

SK is also taking sincere efforts to build a positive atmosphere of healthy competition and sports among the children and youth of the area. Of late it has been found that youth of the area are getting stricken by illness and disease at an early age as they are not taking part in any kind of physical activity. It is in this context, that SK has been organizing racing competition among the youth of Raghopur and Bidupur block. Winners in the sporting event are awarded with cash benefits. Objective of the program is to build an atmosphere of healthy competion among the youth of the area and divert their mind towards constructive activity. Idea was to encourage them to spend their quality time in education and also taking part in sports.

Livelihood & Women’s Empowerment through WSHG Bank Linkage Program

6500 WSHGs(Womens’ Self Help Group) have been formed and strengthened in Vaishali district by Sk. Credit Support & CC facilities from Central Bank of India, Bank of Baroda, Indian Bank, Canara Bank, State Bank of India and Uttar Bihar Grameen Bank has continuously supported these WSHGs for their social and Financial inclusion.

Due to incessant effort of SK & support from Banks, members of WSHGs have been able to mobilize more than Rs.27.32 crores as savings and deposit. Furthermore, around 3500 WSHG's have strengthened their capacity and received credit facility for Income generating activities mainly from Indian Bank, BOB & CBI, which amounts more than Rs. 58.00 crores.

Environmental Sustainability: Plantation Work

As environmental damage, pollution and climate change has emerged as a major problem globally, the Organization has also started making its own contribution in fighting this problem. In line with this, 1000 Mahogany Plants were planted by Samadhan Kendra over 1.5 acres of land in Fatehpur Panchayat of Raghopur Block. Apart from the green cover that these plants will provide in the future, this has caught the attention of the people of the area who have realized the significance and need of plantation to save their environment and their future generation. The echo of this activity has spread to a very wide area and other farmers and villagers have also begun to undertake plantation.
