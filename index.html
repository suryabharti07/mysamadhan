About Samadhan Kendra

Samadhan Kendra was established as a registered Society under the Society Registration Act 1860, based on the philosophy of holistic development—by the people, for the people. We began with the mission to uplift marginalized communities. Our journey is driven by the belief that true progress comes from empowering individuals and communities to shape their own destinies.

We work tirelessly to create sustainable solutions that address the root causes of poverty and inequality, fostering a society where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

Our Story

Our Core Focus Areas

Education

Providing access to quality education, fostering literacy, and empowering the next generation with knowledge and skills for a brighter future.

Sports

Promoting physical well-being, teamwork, and discipline through various sports initiatives, building character and community spirit.

WSHG (Women Self-Help Groups)

Empowering women through self-help groups, fostering economic independence, and promoting social cohesion and leadership.

Environment Protection

Initiating sustainable practices and awareness programs to protect our environment for future generations.

Health

Ensuring access to basic healthcare, promoting hygiene, and conducting health awareness campaigns for community well-being.
