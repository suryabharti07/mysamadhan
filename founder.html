About Me

Age: 44 | Education: Graduate in History

I am Subhash Kumar, a passionate social development professional and the proud founder of Samadhan Kendra, a non-governmental organization dedicated to the upliftment of underprivileged communities in Bihar. My professional journey began with a four-year association with Nehru Yuva Kendra, Hajipur, where I focused on Self-Help Group (SHG) formation—an experience that shaped my commitment to community empowerment.

Motivated by a collective vision for social change, I co-founded Samadhan Kendra with other development-minded individuals. Since then, I have served as the organization’s guiding force, working relentlessly to implement impactful programs across the Vaishali and Saran districts. Under my leadership, the organization has successfully improved the lives of thousands through education, health, environment, livelihood, and youth empowerment initiatives.
